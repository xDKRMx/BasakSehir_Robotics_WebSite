About

Short explanation about Başakşehir Robotics

As Başakşehir Robotics, we are one of the most ambitious candidates in the FRC Competition.
Our mission as Başakşehir Robotics is to provide real-world experiences in technology and engineering to all people around the world.
As for our vision, Başakşehir Robotics aims to equip young people with advanced skills in technology, engineering, and creativity, thereby nurturing future leaders and individuals capable of generating innovative solutions to problems

Deneyap Technologic workshops

The primary goal of Deneyap Technology Workshops is to cultivate future engineers, technology entrepreneurs, and technology leaders who will form the driving force of the National Technology Initiative, which we see as essential for the development of our country. These efforts aim to lay the solid foundations for an independent and strong Turkey.

Our team and Deneyap

Deneyap is an educational initiative launched in 2017 in Turkey to contribute to the development of young individuals with high competency in producing technology. It encompasses technology workshops conducted under the 'Future Technology Stars' program of the Turkey Technology Team Foundation. The education model consists of two phases: the first 24 months of project-based courses and the final 12 months of team period.

A significant part of our team comprises students and instructors from Deneyap. Deneyap Technology Workshops are centers established to contribute to the National Technology Initiative. These workshops offer a 36-month-long free technology education program, which includes 24 months of project-based courses. The program covers 11 different categories including software technologies, cybersecurity, energy technologies, design and manufacturing, mobile applications, electronic programming and the Internet of Things, artificial intelligence, aviation and space technologies, robotics and coding, nanotechnology, and material science. As Deneyap students, we receive comprehensive training in these areas and enhance our skills. We believe that with these skills, we can achieve success in the FRC and create a positive impact in various STEAM fields. Experienced Members: Our team includes mentors and team members who have previously been part of different FRC teams and achieved significant success during that period. This experienced team will provide valuable guidance as we embark on this journey as a new team. Sharing their past experiences among team members will enable us to learn and develop faster. With these characteristics, our team will operate with knowledge and experience that a typical new team might not possess.

Our competitive Social Projects

Our robotics team is working on various exciting projects in the world of technology and science. Our application, Physicolize, offers a 3D and interactive experience that teaches students physics topics in a more fun and understandable way. DreamBot is a colorful and inspiring virtual exhibition designed especially for middle school students to showcase their creativity. With our webinars and hackathon events, we bring together technology enthusiasts to share knowledge and experience. We aim to foster a love for science in young people and encourage them in this field through our science festivals and STEAM education. Additionally, we plan to publish our own magazine to bring scientific topics and research to a wider audience. With our FRC Memorial Forest project, we aim to raise environmental awareness and contribute to nature, while all these projects allow us to both develop our technical skills and make valuable contributions to society.
