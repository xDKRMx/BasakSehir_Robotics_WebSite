As Başakşehir Robotics,
we are shaping the future with technology and creativity.

As Başakşehir Robotics, we are progressing on the path to becoming future technology leaders. We are a meeting point for passionate young people in software development, engineering, and creativity, and we are here to further amplify this passion. In platforms like the Robotics Competition (FRC), we gain real-world experiences and develop our leadership skills through teamwork. Our goal is to invite everyone interested in technology and engineering to join this exciting journey and shape the future together.

Who are we?

Başakşehir Robotics was established in June 2023 by students of the Deneyap Technology Workshops and aimed to be a driving force in Turkey's National Technology Initiative under the umbrella of the T3 Foundation, which was founded in 2018. Our goal as Attila Robotics is to create a positive impact in the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEAM). While doing so, we also aim to introduce the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) to a wider audience. We chose the name Attila Robotics, inspired by Attila, the former ruler of the European Hun Empire. With the name Attila Robotics, we aim to achieve leadership and impressive successes in the world of technology, carrying the name of one of history's powerful leaders. Inspired by Attila's leadership, bravery, and strategic qualities, we are determined to shape the future of robotic innovations.

Our aims

As Başakşehir Robotics, our primary goal is to participate in competitions while adhering to the principles of the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) and achieve success, as well as to leave a positive impact through various works and projects in the STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) field, alongside achieving success.

Our plans

As Başakşehir Robotics, we have many plans and objectives ahead of us. Our plans are not only directed towards the FRC competition but also involve various other robotics and technological competitions. Therefore, we, as Başakşehir Robotics, will move forward with confidence in line with our own plans and the quality of the members we add to our team.

Our vision

Our vision is to bring together talented young people in the STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) field who are ready to unleash their potential and to maximize their capabilities to the fullest.

Başakşehir Spor Kulübü

The owner of name right of the team

Istanbul Başakşehir Football Club was founded on June 4, 2014. Our club is a joint-stock company with seven partners, and its founding president is Göksel Gümüşdağ. In its 6th season in the Süper Lig, Istanbul Başakşehir Football Club became the 6th champion in the history of the Süper Lig. Continuing its journey with the slogan 'Turkey's new champion,' our club is in the same group as European football giants like Manchester United, Paris Saint Germain, and Leipzig in the Champions League during the 2020-2021 season
